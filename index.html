Common Mental Illnesses

Depression

Symptoms: Persistent sadness, loss of interest, changes in appetite, fatigue.

Dangers: Risk of self-harm, severe emotional distress.

Ways to Handle: Therapy, medication, support groups, regular exercise.

Anxiety

Symptoms: Excessive worry, restlessness, fatigue, muscle tension.

Dangers: Impaired daily functioning, panic attacks.

Ways to Handle: Cognitive-behavioral therapy, mindfulness, relaxation techniques.

Activities & Best Practices

  • Practice mindfulness and meditation.
  • Engage in regular physical exercise.
  • Maintain a balanced diet.
  • Establish a healthy sleep routine.
  • Connect with friends and family.
  • Engage in hobbies and interests.
